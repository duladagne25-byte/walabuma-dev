About Walabuma Frontend Dev

Walabuma Frontend Development Study is a premier learning platform dedicated to empowering Ethiopian developers and aspiring frontend engineers. Our mission is to provide high-quality, accessible education in modern web technologies.


Our Vision

To become Ethiopia's leading frontend development community, fostering innovation and technical excellence.


What We Offer

  • ✅ Comprehensive frontend curriculum
  • ✅ Hands-on projects and real-world applications
  • ✅ Mentorship from industry professionals
  • ✅ Community meetups and hackathons
  • ✅ Career guidance and portfolio building

✨ Admin Features: Complete user management system to track registered students.

📍 Ethiopian Focus: Specialized for Ethiopian students with local city selections.
